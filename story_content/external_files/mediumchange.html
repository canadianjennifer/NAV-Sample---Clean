Step 1: What is the scope of the project?

Question Scope Level (1-4)
1. Areas impacted by the change:
How many parts of the organization will be affected by this change? Will it only affect a single department or are the changes expected to impact multiple areas?
2. Number of employees impacted by the change:
How many employees will be impacted by this change?
3. Impact on roles and responsibilities:
Will this project involve changes to peoples’ roles or reporting relationships? Is a reorganization required to support the proposed changes?
4. Impact on business processes:
Will this project result in changes to business processes?
5. Impact on systems and technology change:
How much will technology change?

Question Risk Level (1-4)
1. Case for Change

3. Change Capacity
How much concurrent change are people experiencing/managing?
4. Change Leadership
Are executives/managers willing to undertake the required change leadership/sponsorship role?
